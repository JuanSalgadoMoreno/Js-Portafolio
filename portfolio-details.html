The logo is made up of an Isotype and the letters of the name "Colombian pri house" and its slogan "Food, beer and fun".

A circle ribbon was taken as a base to represent movement and adaptability. In addition to transmitting social life and creativity. Values ​​that the brand tries to communicate.

These elements are unified in such a way that they can be easily recognized, displaying a representative seal of the community.

Branding

In Colombian Pri House I was in charge of creating the logo and look and feel of the brand. In addition to collaborating in the creation of some elements for RRSS. It is a prestigious Colombo Argentina food restaurant-bar, where a space is proposed to socialize and share the general culture and idiosyncrasy of both countries.
